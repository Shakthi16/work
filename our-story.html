OUR STORY

Frost Delights began in 2010, inspired by a vision to create ice creams that blend nostalgia with wellness. Jane Doe, our founder, started in her small-town kitchen, crafting ice creams with fresh dairy and natural ingredients, focusing on both flavor and quality.

2010 - A Dream in a Scoop

Frost Delights began in 2010, inspired by a vision to create ice creams that blend nostalgia with wellness. Jane Doe, our founder, started in her small-town kitchen, crafting ice creams with fresh dairy and natural ingredients, focusing on both flavor and quality.

2012 - The First Parlor

By 2012, Frost Delights opened its first storefront, serving artisanal ice creams that were as wholesome as they were delicious. Customers loved our dedication to real ingredients, and soon, Frost Delights became a local favorite for families seeking treats without compromises.

2015 - Flavors That Care

In 2015, we expanded our flavor range, introducing options that catered to health-conscious customers. From dairy-free sorbets to low-sugar indulgences, Frost Delights aimed to make every treat enjoyable for everyone, regardless of dietary preferences.

2018 - Innovation Meets Health

2018 marked the year we introduced our signature line of "Functional Ice Creams" infused with superfoods like turmeric, matcha, and chia seeds. These creations not only delighted taste buds but also contributed to overall well-being, setting Frost Delights apart as a pioneer in healthy indulgences.

2020 - Sustainability and Health

In 2020, Frost Delights committed to sustainability without compromising health. From compostable packaging to sourcing organic ingredients, every decision was guided by our belief that a better world starts with better choices—even in dessert.

2023 - A Legacy of Joy and Wellness

Today, Frost Delights continues to bring joy with every scoop while keeping health at the forefront. Whether it’s our classic Vanilla Bean Bliss or innovative Green Tea Crunch, every bite reflects our dedication to creating ice creams that nourish both the body and soul.
